The snyk SBT plugin uses the native scala plugins to generate the full
dependency tree of your Scala project. We support sbt-dependency-graph and
coursier from sbt. We also have our own dependency graph generation with the
--sbt-graph option.

For more information see https://snyk.io/docs/snyk-for-scala

Auto detected Files:
  - build.sbt

Examples:
  $ snyk monitor
  $ snyk monitor --file=sub-project/build.sbt
  $ snyk monitor ./sub-project --sbt-graph
  $ snyk monitor -- -Xmx4g
